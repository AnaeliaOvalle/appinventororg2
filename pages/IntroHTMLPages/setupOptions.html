Setting Up App Inventor

App Inventor is all web based, and just needs a bit of installation so that you can test your apps.

Option One (Recommended)

Build apps with an Android device and WiFi Connection

Pros Cons
The quickest way to test and the lowest amount of setup. You have to have an android device. You have to be on a Wifi network that is not heavily firewalled.



Option Two

If you not have an Android device you can use the Emulator.

If you do not have an Android phone or tablet handy, you can still use App Inventor. Have a class of 30 students? Have them work primarily on emulators and share a few devices.

Pros Cons
You do not need a device and can test without a WiFi network Quite a bit of software to download. Because you do not have a device, you cannot test out the accelerometer and other functions that require an actual device.
