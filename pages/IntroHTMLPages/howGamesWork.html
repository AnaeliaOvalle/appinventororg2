Helicopter Game

Game Instructions

Click and hold Left mouse button to go up; release to go down.

How Does The Game Work?

One moves the helicopter by clicking to dodge barriers.

What Happens Throughout The Game?

The barriers become more frequent.

How do you Win?

By beating the current high score.

How do you lose?

By hitting a barrier, ceiling, or floor.

Pop Board Game

Game Instructions

Use the mouse rub out the images before your time runs out.

How Does The Game Work?

You have to erase the images on the board without losing all of your lives within the time limit.

What Happens Throughout The Game?

There are more things that you can hit to lose lives, and order begins to matter.

How do you Win?

By completing each level.

How do you lose?

By losing your lives, by letting the timer run out.

Summary

Games have a way to win and many ways to lose and the game typically get harder as you advance through the game. Games also have instructions and rules.
