What is a Variable?

A variable labels (or names) a piece of text, a list, or a number and can be changed in different places and different times.
Like in the video's example, the variable Tips stood for however much has been made in tips and he uses the tips variable to find out how much was made during a particular hour.


In coding, you define a variable when you need to remember a number, text, and/or when there isn't a component property for storing the information you need. A great example of this come from making the PaintPot app, when you need to control the size of a circle drawn by the user.
